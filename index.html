Our Activities

Weekly Sessions

We organize several sessions on a weekly basis, with multiple meetings depending on the campaigns that are currently running. You can schedule your own session with your friends as well!

Various types of Adventures

It takes all sorts to make a world. There are campaigns with different settings, rules, and characters, so there is fun for everyone. Be sure to express your preferences if you decide to join, we want to know what you like!

DM Mentors

Our DMs are veterans who like to involve as many people as possible in role-playing. For those who want to pursue the tough career of Dungeon Master, there is the opportunity to learn the tricks of the trade in real learning sessions!

Catch us Online!

Do you live far away and cannot physically reach our group? The Discord channel solves every problem! There are channels where you can find people to play with, chat, and participate in sessions organized exclusively online, just so no one is left out!

Session of the Month

Panic at the Gringott!

In a session set in the world of Harry Potter, our adventurers learned that some boys were going to break into the bank, and they had the stern task of stopping them. However, unforeseen situations caused events to evolve in a totally unexpected way...
